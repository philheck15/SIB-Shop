Wholesome, organic and local food

We pride ourselves in serving great food from local producers, hormone- and antibiotic-free beef or chicken burgers, Lebanese wraps with homemade hummus, organic hand-cut fries and old-fashioned milkshake.

Dine-in/Take out & catered box lunches

Our catered box lunch menu is available for pickup or delivery with 24 hr notice. We cater to any dietary restrictions and welcome your needs, please just tell us when you order—we'll be glad to accommodate you with a smile.
