What makes you different makes you beautiful.

Eat like you give a damn

Come on in for some great food from local producers: a burger made with hormone- and antibiotic-free beef or chicken, a Lebanese wrap with homemade hummus, organic hand-cut fries and a milkshake...or just to say hello. Bring the whole family for food you can feel good about. We can't wait to meet you.

Dine-in/Take out & catered box lunches

Learn more about our ingredients. Download a printable version of our daily menu. View our catered box lunch menu—available for pickup or delivery (24 hr notice). Gluten-free? Absolutely. We can serve any sandwich or burger on gluten-free bread or as a lettuce wrap, or just plain. For any dietary restrictions or concerns, please just ask us when you order—we'll do whatever we can to accommodate you and make you smile.
